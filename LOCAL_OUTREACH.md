# ClubHub Victoria Outreach Targets

This is the manual outreach list for first local mentions and backlinks. These are not things the app can post automatically; they require your accounts or direct submissions.

## Priority targets

- Google Business Profile or other local business profiles you already control:
  add `https://clubhubvictoria.ca` as the website link and use `ClubHub Victoria` consistently.
- Reddit `r/VictoriaBC`:
  share only when appropriate and follow subreddit rules. Good use cases are launch posts, “I built a local rec schedule aggregator” posts, or replies when people ask where to find drop-in times.
- Facebook groups for Victoria-area recreation:
  post the site when people ask about drop-in hockey, pickleball, skating, swimming, or rec-centre schedules.
- Local event/directory submissions:
  `https://thingstodovictoria.ca/submit-event/`
- Victoria and region community directories:
  `https://www.victoria-bc.com/community-centers`
  `https://crdcommunitygreenmap.ca/location-type/recreation`

## Ready-to-paste short launch copy

```text
I built ClubHub Victoria to make it easier to find drop-in sports, swims, skates, fitness classes, and rec schedules across Greater Victoria without checking a bunch of separate rec-centre pages.

Site: https://clubhubvictoria.ca
```

## Ready-to-paste slightly longer version

```text
I built ClubHub Victoria to collect public recreation schedules from around Greater Victoria into one place. It’s meant to make it easier to find drop-in sports, swims, skates, fitness classes, and community recreation times without bouncing between rec-centre websites.

If you want to try it:
https://clubhubvictoria.ca
```

## Brand consistency

Use the same name everywhere:

- `ClubHub Victoria`
- `https://clubhubvictoria.ca`

Do not mix in the old `vercel.app` URL when sharing publicly.
